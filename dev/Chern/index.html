TightBindingToolkit.Chern.FindLinks — Method
FindLinks(Ham::Hamiltonian, subset::Vector{Int64}) --> Tuple{Matrix{ComplexF64}, Matrix{ComplexF64}}

Function to get the linking matrices on each neighbouring point in the BZ. On a bond connecting ki and kj, the linking matrix U is defined such that U[m, n] = <v^m[ki]|v^n[kj]> where states[kj[1], kj[2], :, m] v^m[kj], the mth eigenstate at momentum kj.
